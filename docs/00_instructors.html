Investigators

Laura B. Balzer — PI

Associate Professor of Biostatistics, University of California, Berkeley. Developed Two-Stage TMLE and Adaptive Pre-specification; co-led the SEARCH consortium analyses showing TMLE’s gains over standard CRT methods. 2014 Causality in Statistics Education Award.

Mark van der Laan — Co-Investigator

Professor of Biostatistics, University of California, Berkeley. Inventor of TMLE, Super Learner, and several multiple-testing procedures; author of Targeted Learning (Springer, 2011).

Joshua Schwab — Statistical programmer

Maintainer of the TwoStageTMLE R package.
